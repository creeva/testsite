= Nathaniel Oshaben

=== Nathaniel Oshaben

Nathaniel Oshaben received his Bachelor’s Degree in Music Teacher Education from Bowling Green State Univerity in 2003. He continued on and received a Master’s degree in Music Teacher Education and Music Performace (Percussion) from Bowling Green State University in 2005. Mr. Oshaben served as assistant band director for link:{baseurl}/vermilion-high-school-band-directors/kurt-dieringer/[Kurt Dieringer] , starting in 2008 before becoming the head band director in 2010. Mr. Oshaben moved on to another position in 2013, becoming Band Director for Mayfield Schools.
