*Marching Band -- Rain or Shine*

_Marching band started during the hot, sticky summer and marched on through the rainy Fall weather. The band participated in several festivals including ones at Dover and Anthony Wayne. They also hosted their own festival during the Woolybear Weekend. The Marching Band begins preparing for their halftime shows and festivals in mid-July. They practice daily until school begins. The band continues to practice after school hours in order to present a great halftime show. The band marched in several festivals and parades including one in Toronto, the Blossom Festival. It was a great way to end an exciting year of band._
