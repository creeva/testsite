Went through and ran more cabling in the last week. I have the rewiring run for 1 phone jack and a new central access point in the house. Wiring is also done for 2 other jacks -- (50 feet goes by fast). There are 7 more drops to wire up.

On the Coax side of the coin, there are now 4 coax drops plus the central feed runs into the basement. 2 of these are hooked up and operating. There are 8 runs of this left to go.

As far as RJ-45 network drops these have kept me the busiest. So far there are 9 lines running throughout the house. With 8 left to go. After all the wiring is done then the wall panels are slowly going to be switched out and a patch panel is to be installed.
