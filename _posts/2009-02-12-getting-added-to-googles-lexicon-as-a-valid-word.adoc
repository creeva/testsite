What a long strange trip it's been. Back before I started a blog, I was just a person on the link:http://www.starwarsgalaxies.com[swg] forum with the name link:https://creeva.com[Creeva] in his signature. I did a random link:http://google.com[Google] search and I had less than 100 entries, about 3 years ago I was still stuck at 419 entries. These days link:http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&q=creeva&btnG=Google+Search&aq=f&oq=[my number fluctuates for 20k-60K entries in Google] depending on the day. I'm still proud of having the number one entry in Google for "the greatest accomplishment of mankind" and not so happy about being number one for "link:http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&q=afk+entertainer&btnG=Search[afk entertainer]" (UPDATE ON THE SECOND ONE -- I've been bumped to number 2 -- yay me). One thing always bothered me when I started this trip of seeding Google, the fact that originally when I searched for "Creeva" Google sent me back the suggestion "Did you mean _Creeval_?" -- this continued on when I had more hits for Creeva in the database then existed for Creeval.

Every few months I did a variation search on Creeva and normally I would either get no suggestion for some spellings, or I would get something spelled differently. Today however when I did a search for "link:http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&q=Creeeva&btnG=Search[Creeeva]" it came back with the suggestion "did you mean _Creeva_?". Finally, I've broken through the glass ceiling of valid search terms and "Creeva" will start being a valid suggestion.

I really don't care if this changes tomorrow and it goes back to some other suggestion, today I have my moment in the sun.

Granted as a side note, my real name has only 4900 hits for it, and when I search on it, it suggests a different spelling of the last name (different person).
