I'm talking about Barack getting number one. I know that NASA has multiple Twitter accounts, do they follow Barack? What about the Burea of Indian Affairs, are they his link:http://facebook.com[Facebook] friend?

Will they be the ones that think social networks are social networking, in general, so important that they will remember who their friends were before their election and act accordingly?
