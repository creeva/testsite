Last night like hundreds of thousands of people around the states I waited in line for a Nintendo Wii. We arrived at Super K in Lorain, OH to get our chance at the elusive new console. We started waiting at 11 PM for the midnight drawing. Instead of the normal line, Super K was doing a lottery which I assumed would increase my chances without camping out for 2-3 days ahead of time.
