Yesterday during a windstorm we lost power. Well since my wife was out all day, it's more apt to say that I lost power. It's a lot harder for me these days when I lose power than it was when I was a kid. The first thing you think of is that it's ok I have a laptop.

Well, the wifi and other hubs and routers all need electricity -- so no internet.

Ok…… I'll watch a movie on my laptop then, my movies are stored on a powered USB drive……..

How about reading a book….we'll it's getting dark and I'm not sure where the usable and not decorative candles are……

It's very annoying to lose power in the digital age.

Also, did you ever notice that most of the time the power goes out it's at night? That's doubly annoying.
