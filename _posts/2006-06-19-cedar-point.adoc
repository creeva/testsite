Went to Cedar Point at Saturday and we had a blast. Probably the most fun as a group we have all had in over a decade. You never realize that visiting an amusement that was a big part of your day to life as a teenager would be such a trip down memory lane. Before I moved to Oregon we always had season passes so it passed the time and was the same old same old every time we visited.

Now I want a season pass again. Nuff said.

Some disappointing things though about the park -- White water landing seems to be in the stages of deconstruction -- I will miss that ride. I still miss the mill race and the pirate ride.
