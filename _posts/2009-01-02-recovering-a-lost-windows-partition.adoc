A few months ago my home server lost all of my MP3s from a share. I was freaking out thinking some process had magically deleted them. Then I noticed the partition was missing. I spent a few days banging my head against the wall trying to recover the data and nothing helped. Since I'm a listener of link:http://twit.tv/sn[Security Now], I attempted to use link:http://www.grc.com/spinrite.htm[Spin Rite] on the drive, even this didn't help it. I was freaking out.

I set aside the drive and re-addressed it yesterday. A friend of mine had a copy of link:http://www.ptdd.com/[Partition Doctor 3.5] and we ran it against my drive. In a matter of minutes, we managed to get the drive back up and all of the MP3s were there. Neither my wife nor I were too anxious to go through the 2-3 week ripping process we did last time when we re-ripped at a higher bit rate.
