_It was a very good year for the Sailor Marching band. The group expanded its range of performances with a greater variety in its shows, continuing the growth of musicianship and the addition of more flair from the majorettes and the flags._

_Many of the Friday game performances seemed more like synchronized swimming matches with more rain than moonlight. The band also performed in a full complement of festivals as well as in the TV 8 Cleveland Christmas parade. The highlight of the year was their trip and performances in Williamsburg Virginia in the spring._

image::/testsite/images/1997-flag-write.jpg[,role="text-center"]

_The dance camp held at VHS in June let the flag corps and the majorettes know that their season would have a lot more fun and flair. A visiting choreographer taught routines and moves to veterans and novices alike._  +

_In the fall, the squads performed with the marching band, coordinating their dance and moves to the music. After their season was over, they also competed together in the TV8 Christmas Parade and in a competition in Williamsburg featuring squads from all over the United States. The Flag Corps is led byCaptain Shannon Jackson and the Head Majorette is Stacie Moralez._
