*Give me an "M"*

_One of the most enthusiastic supporters of the Conference Championship football team is the Sailor Marching Band. Regardless of the weather, they are always marching and cheering their team to victory._

_The band had a very busy schedule. For each performance, they marched ninety-seven members, nineteen of whom were seniors. They attended festivals at Anthony Wayne, Arcadia, Sandusky, Midpark, Parma, and also marched in the Woolybear Parade._

_Most of the season, excellent weather followed them wherever they traveled. Their own festival was dampened by rain just as it began, but the bands who stuck it out and waited for the rain to pass over were rewarded with a fine evening of entertainment._

_The annual Marching Band Banquet was held on Tuesday, November 17th. The Seniors' last will and testaments were read and the most outstanding Senior awards were presented to Mark Eitzman, Nicki Emery, Eddie McDoniel, and Pam Vance._
