*Orchestra*

The orchestra, under Mr. Hoffman’s guidance, has been very active this year, taking part in commencement, baccalaureate, and entertaining at both the junior and senior class plays.

The county orchestra, well represented by Vermilion musicians, took part in the annual county music festival at Sandusky.

The Vermilion High band, consisting of members chosen from the orchestra, entertained rooters at the home football and basketball games this year, and a large par of it joined with schools to march and play at the Erie County basketball tournament.
