*The Band*

This year the Vermilion High School Band under the direction of Mr. Hoffman, is on the best Vermilion has ever had.

The band played for all home football and basketball games, doing its share in installing into the players the spirit of the occasion. The band also appeared at the Farmers’ Institute.

Thirteen members of the local band are in the Erie County Band. They are: Donald Krapp, Martha Snyder, Charity Moes, Rodney Lewis, Charles Kishman, Charles Schmaltz, Paul Yaggy, Ruth Lingelbach, Janet Parsons, Lucille Hull, Leonard Cipra, Faith Moes, and Marguerite Ball.
