_The Vermilion Sailor Marching Band had a good season in both weather and performances this year. They attended three festivals throughout the season; those being in Anthony Wayne, Sandusky, and Leetonia. The Vermilion Sailor Marching Band Festival was held on Oct. 2 at the stadium with 8 bands attending. Mr. Burt marched 97 members (not including 57 alternates) in all with band director._
