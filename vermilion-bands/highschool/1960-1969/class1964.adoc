*The 1964 Sailor Marching Band*

_"Ladies and Gentlemen, Vermilion High School proudly presents the 1964 Sailor Marching Band under the direction of Mr. William Burt and head drum majorette Miss Linda Harrison._

_After spending a Summer in Chicago, Mr. Burt returned to the band filled with new ideas and marching formations. After practicing innumerable hours, the marching band radiated pep, energy, and school spirit at all football games, where they were featured in half-time shows. The band participated in band festivals in Oberlin and Wellington._

_The Band will resume practice after the spring concert in preparation for a trip to the Lions' International Convention in Toronto._
