=== Breaking Down the Sections To Memorize The Music

To ease the consumption of the of the band history, it has been broken up into several chapters covering the eras and major directors that shaped them. This history is full of images and video references from the Vermilion Sailor Alumni Band YouTube Page. It can be read in any order and can cover just the areas that a reader might be most interested. The chapters with summaries are broken down as follows:

* Introduction: link:/testsite/vermilion-band-history[Now Hear This! Presenting the Vermilion Sailor Marching Band!] 
* Preface: link:/testsite/vermilion-band-history/musical-roots[Vermilion's Musical Roots] covers Vermilion's musical roots, Crystal Beach, Elberta Beach, and Vermilion-On-The-Lake.
* Chapter 1: link:/testsite/vermilion-band-history/pregame[Pregame (1874-1928)] covers the early Vermilion school system, link:/testsite/vermilion-high-school-band-directors/eugene-ackley/[Eugene Ackley], the Erie County Band, and the start of the link:/testsite/vermilion-high-school-orchestra/[Vermilion Orchestras].
* Chapter 2: link:/testsite/vermilion-band-history/marching-into-the-stadium[Marching Into The Stadium (1929 - 1955)] goes over the link:/testsite/vermilion-high-school-band-directors/james-hoffman/[James Hoffman] era, the first majorettes, the conversion of the link:/testsite/vermilion-high-school-orchestra/[Vermilion Orchestras] to the Vermilion Concert Band, the start of the link:/testsite/vermilion-bands/highschool/[Vermilion Sailor Marching Band], link:/testsite/songs/fight-song-anchors-away/[Fight Song], and link:/testsite/songs/alma-mater/[Alma Mater].
* Chapter 3: link:/testsite/vermilion-band-history/fanfares[Fanfares (1955 - 1979)] details the link:/testsite/vermilion-high-school-band-directors/william-burt/[William Burt] timeline, the roots of the marching bands style, notable performances, the historic marching band uniform, the start of many traditions, Vermilion festivals, and the Ducky.
* Chapter 4: link:/testsite/vermilion-band-history/stepping-off[Stepping Off (1979 - 1994)] covers the link:/testsite/vermilion-high-school-band-directors/david-henry/[David Henry] era, a breakdown of the components that goes into a Vermilion Sailor Marching Band Show, corporate sponsorship, the band picnic, Tommy Perkins, the Cleveland Christmas Parade, the founding of the link:/testsite/vermilion-bands/alumni/[Vermilion Sailor Alumni Band], and Soundsations adding live instrumentation.
* Chapter 5: link:/testsite/vermilion-band-history/the-feature-down-in-front[The Feature Down In Front (1994 - 2007)] details link:/testsite/vermilion-high-school-band-directors/joseph-price/[Joseph Price]'s leadership, the changing of uniforms, the long term contribution of the Color Guard, the Madrigral Festival, link:/testsite/vermilion-high-school-band-directors/william-burt/[William Burt]'s final alumni show, and Winter Guard.
* Chapter 6: link:/testsite/vermilion-band-history/a-kaleidoscope-drill[A Kaleidoscope Drill (2007 - 2016)] covers the timeline for the directorships of link:/testsite/vermilion-high-school-band-directors/kurt-dieringer/[Kurt Dieringer],link:/testsite/vermilion-high-school-band-directors/nathaniel-oshaben/[Nathaniel Oshaben], link:/testsite/vermilion-high-school-band-directors/mike-komperda/[Mike Komperda], and link:/testsite/vermilion-high-school-band-directors/rachel-caldwell/[Rachel Caldwell]. The band changed to a corp style, added Future Sailor Night, and Winter Guard starts to succeed in a big way.
* Chapter 7: link:/testsite/vermilion-band-history/high-step[High Step (2016-Current)] details the arrival of link:/testsite/vermilion-high-school-band-directors/kimberly-judd/[Kimberly Judd], pushback from the community on changes to the band, and a leadership that cements the future success of the Vermilion Band Program.
* Epilogue: link:/testsite/vermilion-band-history/victory-march[Victory March] focuses on the overall success of the band program and the Vermilion High School Hall of Fame.

_When I initially delved into this piece of writing, I believed I had a good grasp of most of its components. I felt confident that I had gathered sufficient information from yearbooks, articles, and various sources over the span of a decade, providing a comprehensive understanding of the band's history. I had a wealth of yearbooks and an extensive collection of photographs at my disposal, not to mention hours of video footage spanning from the 1980s to the present day. However, what I had not thoroughly explored were the newspaper articles, and this turned out to be a significant oversight._

_As I combed through the newspaper archives, I came across several pieces of information that challenged long-held beliefs and contradicted certain facts that had been repeated for decades. It became apparent that some memories were flawed, and there were instances of individuals mistakenly taking credit for certain achievements. Perhaps some people only knew half of the story, or they had been misinformed. Regardless, it is crucial for the accuracy and integrity of the above writing that I make adjustments and updates based on these newfound facts._

_I am committed to ensuring the accuracy of the band's history, and I welcome any new information or corrections that may arise from further research. The goal is to present a comprehensive and truthful account of the Vermilion Marching Band's journey and the contributions of its members throughout the years._
